A Different Approach To Vocabulary

Based on a linguistic study that concluded "Learning the spellings of words provides a big boost in learning their pronunciations and meanings", we created a spelling game that shows the definition of the word you're spelling after you've gotten it right.

Customizable Learning

With our game, you can create and save your own custom flashcard decks and track the progress of your study session. The game follows a reinforced learning logic where a word spelled wrong will be continiously presented until it is spelled right.

Kid-Friendly

Easy to understand and highly visual game layout with a base dictionary that allow kids to learn new words and what they mean. A great way for kids to study for their grade school spelling tests!
